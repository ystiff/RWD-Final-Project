Sisters | Twinny

I have an eldest sister, her name is Mona, the one at the right side of the image. She is very flexible and smart. I thought so many things from her, though having her as a sister is a pressure but it was for my own good. Walking to my journey in college without them are hard, but the lesson that they equip to me are there to guide me. By the way, at the left side I have my sister named Shane. She is so beautiful and I adore her for that. She wants me to pursue the things that she didn't do in her life. Our bonding are watching some movies, playing chess, biking, drawing, swimming and throwing some jokes. She was actually an adventurer. She always try and want me to go out of my shell. I learn to love other countries because of them. I am now looking forward and widen my perspective in various area with emphathy and holistic view. Change, changes me.
